It came back. Five months later. Just five months after me thinking this is [unlikely to happen again](https://danieln.uk/a-night-to-remember/), at least here in my own back garden. But here we are, once again a clear night with an almost new moon and a brilliant Aurora display. Naked eye visible once more, with the sky turning visibly red across the entire Northern horizon.

![](https://cdn.danieln.uk/danielnuk/static/images/DSC01744-Enhanced-NR.jpg)

Next time (hopefully there is a next time!) I will remember to bring my battery pack for the lens warmer; dew was becoming a real problem after midnight and was a constant distraction with having to wipe the lens between shots. A minor complaint!
